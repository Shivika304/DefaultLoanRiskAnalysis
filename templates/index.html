THE PROCESS

How LoanGuard Works

01

Enter Borrower Data

Input financial details: income, loan amount, DTI ratio, credit history, and more. New customers with no credit history are handled separately.

02

AI Analysis

Our Random Forest model — 200 decision trees — evaluates all 11 risk factors simultaneously, just like an experienced credit analyst would.

03

Risk Assessment

Receive a detailed risk score, probability breakdown, visual analysis, and the top factors that drove the decision — fully explainable.

04

Lending Decision

Get a clear recommendation: Approve, Manual Review, or Deny — with actionable steps for borderline and high-risk applicants.

LOW RISK

Loan Approved

Default probability ≤ 30%. Strong credit profile — proceed with standard loan terms.

MODERATE RISK

Manual Review

Default probability 30–55%. Conditional approval with higher rate, collateral, or co-signer.

HIGH RISK

Loan Denied

Default probability > 55%. Risk of loss exceeds expected returns. Application rejected.
